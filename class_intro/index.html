Keep Calm.

A lot of this stuff will be brand new to you. It can be intimidating, but I got your back. If you are unclear on any concepts, even if it's May and you still don't understand stuff from today, don't hestitate to ask me any questions. It's how you learn!

But before you ask me...

  1. Always consult Google first. 99% of your questsions are already answered by at least a dozen other people on the web.
  2. Ask a teddy bear. And make sure you explain every detail - your teddy bear is an inanimate object with limited cognitive ability.
  3. No, I'm serious. The teddy bear thing works. Half of the time you'll answer your own question by phrasing it to someone who doesn't know what you're talking about.
  4. OK, now you can ask me.
